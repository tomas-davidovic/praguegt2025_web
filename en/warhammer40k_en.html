Warhammer 40.000

Lukáš "Ulrik" Seidel

Hi,
my name is Lukáš "Ulrik" Seidel. I've been playing Warhammer since 1999 and started with the Space Wolves. That's been my main faction until now.

I'm what you might call a casual tournament player. For over ten years I've been organizing both regular competitive league events, large two-day tournaments, and events designed for complete beginners.

At PragueGT I am the main organizer for the Warhammer 40k section. My work at the tournament will mainly consist of coordinating all necessary things, checking registrations, and during the event itself, supervising the smooth running and solving any problems.
